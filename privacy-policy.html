Privacy Policy

Eclipse Bot  ·  Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Eclipse Bot ("the Bot") is a Discord bot designed to enhance your server experience with moderation, leveling, music, logging, and more. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

By adding Eclipse Bot to your Discord server or interacting with it, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

Data We Collect

Eclipse Bot may collect and temporarily process the following data to function correctly:

  • Discord User IDs (to track XP, levels, roles, and moderation actions)
  • Discord Server (Guild) IDs (to store server-specific settings)
  • Message content (only when required for automod or chat features)
  • Voice channel activity (for music and voice logging features)
  • Usernames and nicknames (for logging and onboarding displays)

How We Use Your Data

All data collected is used solely to provide the Bot's features:

  • Leveling system — tracking XP and rank per user per server
  • Moderation — recording bans, kicks, and warnings
  • Auto-moderation — detecting rule-breaking messages
  • Logging — posting server event logs to designated channels
  • Onboarding & Welcome — greeting new members
  • Role management — assigning and removing roles automatically

Data Storage

Eclipse Bot stores data in-memory during runtime. Persistent data (such as XP and moderation records) may be stored in a database accessible only to the bot owner.

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as the Bot is present in your server. If you remove the Bot from your server, you may request deletion of all associated data by contacting the bot owner via Discord.

Children's Privacy

Eclipse Bot does not knowingly collect data from users under the age of 13. If you believe a minor has provided data to the Bot, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

Your Rights

  • Request a copy of the data stored about you
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Opt out by removing the Bot from your server

Contact

For any privacy-related concerns, data deletion requests, or questions, please contact the bot owner directly on Discord or via your server's support channel.
